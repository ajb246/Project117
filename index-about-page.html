About the Artist

Milena Narvaez is a visual artist based in New Jersey, specializing in painting, ceramics, and sculpture. Her work reflects a deep connection to nature, emotion, and cultural roots.She explores themes of identity, transformation, and resilience through a diverse range of mediums. With a growing portfolio, Milena continues to challenge artistic boundaries and inspire through vibrant, expressive creations. Looking to inspire the future, Milena is dedicated to teaching and mentoring others in the world of Art.
